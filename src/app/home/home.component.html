What does this app do?

  • You can set up tournaments to play 1 vs 1's in the swiss format.
  • Create players that can participate in the tournaments.
  • Create a tournament and assign players to it.
  • Start the tournament, the app will take care of the matchmaking.
  • Enter results of the matches and proceed to the next rounds.
  • Finish the tournament, the app will provide rankings and statistics.
